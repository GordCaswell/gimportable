Figure 14.131.  The “Colors/Auto” submenu


The Auto submenu contains operations which automatically adjust the distribution of colors in the active layer, without requiring any input from the user. Several of these operations are actually implemented as plugins.

11.6.1.  Activate submenu

  • You can access this submenu from the image window through Colors → Auto.

11.6.2.  Contents of the “Auto” Submenu

The Auto submenu contains the following entries:

  • Section 11.7, “ Equalize ”

  • Section 11.8, “ White Balance ”

  • Section 11.9, “ Color Enhance ”

  • Section 11.10, “ Normalize ”

  • Section 11.11, “ Stretch Contrast ”

  • Section 11.12, “ Stretch HSV ”
