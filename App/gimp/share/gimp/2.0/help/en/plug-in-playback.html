13.2.1.  Overview

This plug-in lets you play an animation from a multi-layers GIF, MNG or even XCF image, to test it.

13.2.2.  Activate the filter

You can find this filter through Filters → Animation → Playback

13.2.3.  Options

Figure 15.260.  “Playback” filter options


This dialog has :

Preview

This preview of the animation automatically fits the frame size. The number of the displayed frame is shown below the preview.

Buttons

Three buttons are available:

Play/Stop

Play/Stop to play or stop the animation.

Rewind

Rewind to re-launch the animation from start.

Step

Step to play the animation step by step.
